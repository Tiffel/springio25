Barcelona

The Palau de Congressos de Barcelona is a large conference centre situated in an atmospheric and picturesque location in Barcelona called Montjuïc.

City communications are excellent and access from Barcelona’s Airport and Sants Station is very quick.

Barcelona is a beautiful and vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Spain. It offers a wide variety of interesting places to visit and activities to enjoy, such as the famous Sagrada Familia by Gaudí, Parc Güell, the Barcelona Cathedral, and the Gothic Quarter.
